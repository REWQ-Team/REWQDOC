# Privacy Policy for REWQ App

**Last Updated: December 2, 2025**

## Overview

REWQ App ("the App") is a simple image viewer and slideshow application that operates entirely on your local device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

REWQ App does **NOT** collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

- ❌ No personal identification information
- ❌ No usage analytics or telemetry
- ❌ No crash reports sent to external servers
- ❌ No tracking cookies or identifiers
- ❌ No network connections to external services
- ❌ No advertising or third-party integrations

## Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **only on your local device**:

### 1. Application Settings
- **Location**: `%AppData%\REWQ\settings.json`
- **Contents**:
  - Display interval preferences
  - Repeat playback settings
  - Cache size preferences
  - File sorting preferences
  - Language selection
  - Recent folders and files list
  - Recent session files list

### 2. Session Files
- **Location**: User-selected location (when you save a session)
- **Contents**:
  - List of image file paths
  - Current image index
  - Source type (folder or files)

### 3. Log Files
- **Location**: `%AppData%\REWQ\Logs\`
- **Contents**:
  - Application startup/shutdown events
  - Error messages (file access errors, image loading errors)
  - File paths accessed by the application
- **Retention**: Logs older than 30 days are automatically deleted
- **Purpose**: Troubleshooting and debugging only

## Your Image Files

- The App only accesses image files that **you explicitly select**
- Image files are read from your local storage only
- No copies of your images are created or transmitted anywhere
- The App uses temporary memory cache for performance, which is cleared when the App closes

## Permissions Required

The App requires the following Windows permissions:

- **File System Access**: To read image files you select
- **Local Storage**: To save application settings and session files

## Data Security

- All data remains on your local device
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- No cloud storage or external servers are used
- Settings and logs are stored in standard Windows application data folders

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## Third-Party Services

The App does **NOT** use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

## Your Rights

Since the App does not collect any personal information:

- There is no data to request, modify, or delete from our servers
- All data is under your control on your local device
- You can delete all App data by:
  1. Uninstalling the App
  2. Manually deleting the folder: `%AppData%\REWQ\`
